Welcome to the Gift-a-wish website.

Have you ever struggled to buy the perfect gift for a loved one, even after knowing them forever? Want to make your next gift a memorable one? With our new website we aim to ease your gift-buying stress by helping you find the perfect gift based on your hobbies and interests. Gift-a-wish acts as your gift buying assistant, it helps you connect with your friends and family which enables you to view a bit more about them, keep track of key events such as birthdays and a generator that gives gift suggestions. Our main goal is to help people use technology to enrich their daily lives, and with this website you can strengthen those meaningful relationships starting with a thought out gift!
